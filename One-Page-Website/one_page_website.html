With the world full of survival building games, it can be difficult to choose which ones to play. The one that’s really caught my attention is Satisfactory. This is the sort of game that makes me feel like I discovered gaming all over again, to feel like a kid who just discovered video games. This is a factory building game in which you play the part of a pioneer who arrives on an alien planet via an orbital drop pod. You land safely on the planet in one of 4 predetermined drop locations, each with its own unique biomes. Here you start by salvaging your drop pod and are quickly encouraged to build a base hub. From here, you begin to build the most basic building blocks of your factory. In this game you get to build just about anywhere, in any way you see fit. Your imagination is the only thing that can limit what you can do. You’ll build massive assembly lines, explore a huge map that takes you through many biomes, including meadows, forests, swamp, desert, and other more alien landscapes all the while navigating various alien creatures. This game is highly recommended, especially if you love the logistical approach to survival building. Check it out!
